Directed Reading Program


During my Spring 2024 Co-op term, I worked on a project as part of Waterloo Women in Mathematic's Directed Reading Program!
With three other undergrad students, we worked with a C&O grad student to explore Tyshkevich Decompositions,
a way to decompose graphs into unique pieces (kinda like prime factorization).

Our final presentation is linked below! My partner and I's section starts at slide 13!!



I'll be participating in the Directed Research Project in Fall 2024, conducting original research into Tyshkevich Decompositions and other graph decompositions!
